About the BMW M4

The BMW M4 is a high-performance coupe known for aggressive design and powerful driving dynamics.

Inspired by BMW’s racing heritage, the M4 offers a perfect balance between everyday usability and track-ready performance.

Horsepower: 503 HP

0–60 mph: 3.8 sec
