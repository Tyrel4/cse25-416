About the BMW M2

The BMW M2 is a compact high-performance sports coupe designed for driving enthusiasts. It delivers strong power, agile handling, and a true motorsport feel.

Inspired by BMW’s racing heritage, the M2 offers a perfect balance between everyday usability and track-ready performance.

Specifications

Engine: 3.0L Twin-Turbo Inline-6

Horsepower: 453 HP

0–60 mph: 4.1 sec

Top Speed: 250 km/h
