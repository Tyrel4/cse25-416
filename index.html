BMW M Series

History of BMW M

BMW M was founded in 1972 as the motorsport division of BMW. The first M road car was the legendary BMW M1 in 1978. Over time, BMW developed high-performance versions of many models including the M2, M3, M4, M5 and M8. Today BMW M cars combine racing technology with luxury and everyday usability.
