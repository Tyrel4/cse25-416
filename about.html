About the owner

Founded in 1916 as an aircraft engine manufacturer, Bayerische Motoren Werke (BMW) transitioned to motorcycles in 1923 and automobiles in 1928. Based in Munich, Germany, the company evolved from producing small, licensed cars to becoming a global leader in premium, high-performance luxury vehicles and engines.
